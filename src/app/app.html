About Audilator

Audilator is a free calculator built with Flutter that uses on-device speech recognition to let you calculate math expressions and convert units entirely with your voice.

Whether you need to convert kilometers to miles, calculate a percentage, or solve any math expression — just speak naturally and get your answer instantly. No typing needed.
